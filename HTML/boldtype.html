The Bold Type


Genre :- Dramedy(comedy + drama)
IMDB Rating :- 7.9/10
Rating :- 4.5/5

The bold type is very much like sex education but a little more women centric.Don't know what's sex education?okay i pity you *sigh*.The bold type revolves around the lives of mainly the three lead roles,all three females.HOW AMAZING?.namely,Sutton Brady, a wannabe stylist with no background in fashion,Jane Sloan,a budding writer who also lost her mother to breast cancer as a kid and last but not the least Kat Edison,The head of socials at Scarlet.

The three end up being best friends after being united by working for Scarlet.People at Scarlet are here to make a difference just like any of us?right?.And the best way to do it is through there magazine.Now,like any other women's magazine or dare i say just magazine,Scarlethas a few branches to work for and our dear protagonists come from 3 different major branches.Sutton who started off as an assistant to one of the editors looks for a break in the fashion department.While Jane has just started working as a writer after 4 years of work as an intern.And Kat,who probably got there because of her father but when it comes to raising a voice for the change she wants to see,does she still need guidance? i think not!

As three very young girls,with rebellious energy and heart at their right place,they figure life out in the most bustling city of the world,New York,in a world where feminism has just begun taking the front seat,how hard is it to lead life as the rightful feminist.If you have encountered the same question in your life i am sure it's going to be a pretty relatable ride.

But aside from this,to make a difference while you work under someone,hmm sounds difficult,right?But not when you have got yourself bosses like Jaqueline Carlyle,Richard Hunter,Oliver Grayson and a little underrated Lauren.When your bosses think just like you and only have maturity to add to your actions its hardly possible that you might go wrong.Yes,by the sound
it all Scarlet,does seem like a dream job,but before you end up thinking it's easy..which will be more than just highly mistaken,join the ride and watch this 5 seasons and 55 episodes long amazing series which is worthy of all the appreciation it has received.until next time <3
